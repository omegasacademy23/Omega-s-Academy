  • Excel Fundamentals: Launching Excel, interface overview, ribbon customization, workbook structure & shortcuts

  • Modifying Worksheets: Move/copy data, insert/delete rows & columns, resize cells, manage sheets
  • Formatting Data: Fonts, borders, number formats, conditional formatting & styles
  • Charts & SmartArt: Insert images, shapes, SmartArt, create & format charts
